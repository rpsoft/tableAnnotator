Observed data are presented for the EuroQoL 5-Dimension 3-Level Questionnaire (EQ-5D-3 L). At week 16, patients initially randomized to placebo who were non-responders switched to secukinumab treatment. Results are not shown for patients who continued on placebo after week 16.

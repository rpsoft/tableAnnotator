FEV1 denotes forced expiratory volume in 1 second, and FVC forced vital capacity. † The body-mass index is the weight in kilograms divided by the square of the height in meters. ‡ The listed value is the prednisone equivalent. § Reversibility was measured at baseline. ¶ The percent of the predicted value before bronchodilation was assessed at the screening visit.

Figure 1. Effect of Rosuvastatin on Postoperative Atrial Fibrillation, Overall and According to Prespecified Subgroups
Shown are odds ratios for the incidence of atrial fibrillation after cardiac surgery (assessed by 5-day Holter electrocardiographic monitoring) among patients assigned to rosuvastatin as compared with those assigned to placebo. After randomization, surgery was canceled for 28 patients in the rosuvastatin group and for 20 in the placebo group. Data on the troponin I concentration at baseline were missing for 53 patients in the rosuvastatin group and for 48 in the placebo group. The denominator for coronary-artery bypass grafting (CABG) in-cludes only patients who had CABG without aortic-valve replacement, and the denominator for aortic-valve replacement includes only those who had aortic-valve replacement without CABG. A total of 26 patients in the rosuvastatin group and 27 in the placebo group had both CABG and aortic-valve replacement, and an additional 9 patients in the rosuvastatin group and 13 in the placebo group had neither CABG nor aortic-valve replacement but instead had some other type of surgery (out of the total of 10 patients in the rosuvastatin group and 15 in the placebo group who had other types of surgery; see Table S2 in the Supplementary Appendix). Of those who had surgery, data on glucocorticoid and nonsteroidal antiinflammatory drug (NSAID) use were missing for 34 patients in the rosuvastatin group and for 31 in the placebo group. For each prespecified subgroup, squares represent odds ratios, with the size of the square proportional to the amount of statistical information, and horizontal lines represent 95% confidence intervals. For the analysis that included all patients, the odds ratio and 95% confidence intervals are represented by a diamond, with values and results of the significance test presented along-side. A square or diamond to the left of the vertical line indicates a benefit with rosuvastatin, but the benefit is significant at the 5% level only if the horizontal line or diamond does not overlap the vertical line. The arrow indicates that the 95% confidence interval exceeds 2.0

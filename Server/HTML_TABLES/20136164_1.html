Fig. 1. Low-dose treatment group effects on key abnormal lipids in mixed dyslipidemia. Effects of 12-week treatment with fenofibric acid 135 mg monotherapy, low-dose statin monotherapy, or fenofibric acid + low-dose statin combination therapy on mean percentage changes in high-density lipoprotein cholesterol (HDL-C), triglycerides (TG), and low-density lipoprotein cholesterol (LDL-C). Mean values at baseline and final visit are also presented as mg/dL (mmol/L). p-Values are shown for statistically significant differences, comparing combination therapy with fenofibric acid monotherapy or low-dose statin monotherapy. One patient with extreme outlying percentage change values was excluded from the HDL-C and TG analyses. SE = standard error.

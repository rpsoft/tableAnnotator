The percentages are the observed rates of the composite primary end point at 96 hours. For each subgroup, the size of the square is proportional to the number of patients and represents the odds ratio estimate for the treatment effect with adjustment for Cochran–Mantel–Haenszel analysis. The horizontal lines indicate 95% confidence intervals. The overall treatment effect in the early-eptifibatide group, as compared with that in the delayed-eptifibatide group, is represented by the diamond, and the dashed vertical line represents the corresponding overall odds ratio point estimate.

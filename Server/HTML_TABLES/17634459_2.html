Figure 2 (facing page). Efficacy of Maintenance Therapy with Certolizumab Pegol in Patients with a Response to Induction Therapy at Week 6. Panel C shows the response over time in patients in the intention-to-treat population who had a response to induction therapy at week 6, as well as the response in the subgroup of patients with a CRP level of at least 10 mg per liter. Panel D shows the percentage of patients with clinical remis-sion (defined as a CDAI score ≤150) at week 26 in the intention-to-treat population and in the subgroup of those with baseline CRP serum levels of at least 10 mg per liter.

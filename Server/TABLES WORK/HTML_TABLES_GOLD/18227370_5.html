Figure 4. Comparisons of doxazosin mesylate with chlorthalidone. Data are expressed as hazard ratios (95% confidence intervals) (with event rates [SEs] and numbers of events given for each drug) by race and metabolic/cardiometabolic syndrome (MetS) status.
